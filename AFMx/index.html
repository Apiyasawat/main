AF Management
10 things to Remember
HeartRhythmBox.com
1. AF does not have to be "IRREGULAR".
AF may be regular in patients with impaired AV nodal conduction or in faster V pacing.
2. Rhythm control should be considered urgently in...
AF with extremely fast conduction to accessory pathway (AF w/WPW), AF with end-organ damage such as STEMI or acute pulmonary edema.
