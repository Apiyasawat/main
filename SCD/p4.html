Cigarettes | Medications | Fish Oil
Smoking increases risk of SCD by 2.4 times.
Risk of SCD is unrelated to the amount and duration of smoking. The risk is normalized immediately after quitting.
Goldenberg et al. Arch Intern Med 2003.

Aldosterone Antagonist reduce inflammation 29% in RALES
